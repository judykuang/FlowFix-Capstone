🚿 Water Heaters

Yes, FlowFix specializes in both traditional tank-style and modern tankless water heaters. Our technicians can perform routine maintenance, such as flushing the tank to remove sediment, or handle complex repairs like replacing heating elements and thermostats. If your unit is beyond repair, we offer professional installation services to help you select and set up a new, energy-efficient model.

Most water heaters have a lifespan of 8 to 12 years. Key signs that it's time for a replacement include rusty or discolored water, strange banging or rumbling noises from the tank, and frequeny leaking around the base. If you find yourself running out of hot water much faster than usual or notice a significant spike in your energy bills, your unit likely needs an upgrade.
